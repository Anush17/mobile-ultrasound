About Us

Get the ultrasound studies you need when you need them. We are experienced in all applications of ultrasound.

This service will allow us to bring the diagnostics science of ultrasound directly to you and your patients, wherever needed. The revolutionary, digital hand ultrasound machine rivals the image quality of much larger machines while being similar in size and appearance to a laptop computer.
